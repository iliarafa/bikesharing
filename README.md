# bikesharing

Most trips start and end in midtown and downtown. This is a result of New Yorkers commuting to and from work and it will be reinforced when we observe graphs related to trips by our. Increased traffic is also observed in specific sites of Upper East and Upper West Side. We see the marks increase in size outside the Metropolitan Museum of Art, the Gugggenheim Museum and the Natural History Museum. This in its turn is a result of tourists visiting major city sites.
